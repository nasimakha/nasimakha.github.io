About Me

I am an Ofsted registered Childminder with over 30 years' experience. My current Ofsted rating is 'Good' and I attend regular training sessions to keep myself up to date with changes in legislation regarding EYFS and safe-guarding issues. I am also qualified as a Nursery Nurse and have experience of being a Childminder Mentor, assisting new Childminders.

I live in a semi-detached house in Brimsdown, Enfield which has a large, dedicated playroom as well as a fully-secured garden for the children to play and explore in. It has easy access to local transport such as bus stations and Brimsdown railway station and is also next to Durants park where regular visits are made.

I have worked with a range of different children, including those with special needs. I work with qualified assistants, have no pets and have a strict no smoking policy. All children in my care are treated as individuals and are encouraged to reach their full potential.

I offer 15 and 30 hours of free, funded childcare for eligible 9 month-old infants, and two, three and four year old children. I do not charge anything extra towards food, nappies and other expenses. However, there is a £7 per hour surplus for any extra hours in addition to the free, funded hours. Funded hours can be used for either all term time, or stretched throughout the year.
