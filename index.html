About Me

I am an Ofsted registered Childminder with many years' experience. I am a member of NCMA and a member of Enfield Childminding Network with the Children Come First Quality Assured Scheme. I have worked with a range of different children, including those with special needs. I work with qualified assistants, have no pets and have a strict no smoking policy.

As a member of Enfield Childminding Network I am subject to unannounced visits as well as having regular support and monitoring visits; I also attend regular training sessions throughout the year. My weekly learning activity plan covers the EYFS curriculum - and this includes all areas of child development. All children in my care are treated as individuals and are encouraged to reach their full potential.

I live in a semi-detached house in Brimsdown, Enfield with my husband and four children. It has easy access to local transport, such as Brimsdown Rail Station, and is next to Durants Park where regular visits are made to. I also take the children to the children's centre for play sessions and we also visit the library and toddler group regularly. I have a purpose-built, fully-resourced playroom and a fully-enclosed, safe and secure outdoor play area.

All fees are negotiable and discounts are given for siblings. I also do school pick-ups from: Brimsdown School, Eastfield School, St James School, St Barnabas Pre-School, and Kingfisher Hall.
